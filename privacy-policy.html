Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 19, 2026

Thank you for using Mon-Eng Dictionary ("we," "our," or "the App"). Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our mobile application.

Key Points:
  • We do not collect, store, or share any personal information from users
  • No account registration or login is required
  • The app displays third-party advertisements through Google AdMob
  • All dictionary data is stored locally on your device

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Personal Information

We do not collect any personal information from users. Our app does not require:

  • Account registration or login
  • Email addresses
  • Names or contact information
  • Location data
  • Device identifiers
  • Any other personal data

1.2 Local Data Storage

The App stores the following data locally on your device only:

  • Dictionary entries and word definitions
  • Your favorite/bookmarked words
  • App settings and preferences (language settings, font size)
  • Search history (stored locally and never transmitted)

This data remains on your device and is never transmitted to our servers or any third parties.

2. Third-Party Advertising

2.1 Google AdMob

Our App displays advertisements using Google AdMob, a third-party advertising service. Google AdMob may collect and use certain information to provide personalized advertisements. This information may include:

  • Device advertising identifiers (IDFA on iOS)
  • IP address
  • Device type and operating system
  • App usage data
  • Ad interaction data

2.2 Google's Data Collection

Google AdMob's data collection and use is governed by Google's Privacy Policy. For more information about how Google collects and processes data, please review:

  • Google Privacy Policy
  • Google AdMob Partner Guidelines
  • Google Ad Settings - Control personalized ads

2.3 Opting Out of Personalized Ads

You can control personalized advertising on your iOS device:

  • Go to Settings → Privacy & Security → Tracking
  • Disable "Allow Apps to Request to Track"
  • Or go to Settings → Privacy & Security → Apple Advertising
  • Enable "Limit Ad Tracking"

3. How We Use Information

Since we do not collect any personal information, we do not use, process, or share any user data. All app functionality operates entirely on your device.

4. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not share, sell, rent, or trade any user information with third parties because we do not collect any personal data. The only third-party service integrated into our app is Google AdMob for displaying advertisements, which operates under Google's own privacy policies.

5. Data Security

Since all your data (favorites, settings, search history) is stored locally on your device using iOS's secure storage mechanisms:

  • Your data is protected by your device's security features
  • Data is not transmitted over the internet
  • Only you have access to your app data through your device
  • If you delete the app, all local data is removed from your device

6. Children's Privacy

Our App does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 years of age. Since we do not collect any personal data at all, the App is safe for users of all ages. However, please note that third-party advertisements from Google AdMob are displayed, which are subject to Google's policies regarding age-appropriate advertising.

7. International Data Transfers

Since we do not collect or transmit any user data, there are no international data transfers. However, Google AdMob may transfer data internationally as described in their privacy policy.

8. Your Rights and Choices

Regarding the data stored locally on your device, you have full control:

  • Access: All your data is accessible within the app
  • Delete: You can delete favorite words within the app, or delete all data by uninstalling the app
  • Modify: You can change settings and preferences at any time in the Settings tab
  • Export/Backup: Your data is stored in your device's app data, which can be backed up through iOS backup features

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted in the App and on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date. We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes. Your continued use of the App after any modifications indicates your acceptance of the updated Privacy Policy.

10. Third-Party Links

Our App does not contain links to external websites or services. All functionality is contained within the app itself.

11. Analytics and Tracking

We do not use any analytics services or tracking tools. We do not track how you use the app, which words you search for, or any other usage patterns. The only tracking that occurs is through Google AdMob for advertising purposes, as described in Section 2.

12. California Privacy Rights (CCPA)

For California residents: Since we do not collect any personal information, the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA) provisions regarding data access, deletion, and sale do not apply. However, you have the right to control advertising tracking as described in Section 2.3.

13. European Privacy Rights (GDPR)

For users in the European Economic Area (EEA): Since we do not collect or process any personal data, GDPR obligations regarding data processing do not apply to our direct operations. However, Google AdMob's data processing may be subject to GDPR, and you can exercise your rights through Google's privacy controls.
